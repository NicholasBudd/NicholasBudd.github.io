The Global Effect of COVID-19

Visualize and analyze the impact COVID-19 had on the global scale.

December 29, 2021
SUMMARY

Several metrics of COVID-19 are analyzed in this map focused Tableau dashboard. The data spans from Jan 2020 to Nov 2021 and is sourced from OurWorldInData. The main idea behind this study was to get as much information without any bias on the death rate behind the pandemic that took the world by storm. Let's take a look.



BACKGROUND
The raw data is sourced courtesy of OurWorldInData and includes global data from hundreds of countries from the beginning of the pandemic, around Jan. 2020 all the way up to Nov. 2021.

The second part of our dashboard is breaking the data down to see total death count per continent.
