      Seattle Air Bnb Statistics

      Visualize and analyze when and why prices fluctuate for Air Bnbs in Seattle.

      December 29, 2021


      Several metrics of the Seattle housing market in relation to Air Bnb's are analyzed in this map focused Tableau dashboard. The data is from 2016 and is sourced from Kaggle. Feel free to zoom into different zip codes in the bottom left portion of the dashboard to get a better feel for Seattle Air Bnbs and what areas seem to be the hottest. You can use the bottom right line graph to find at what point in the year your Air Bnb will give you the best return on your money as well as other indicators that might help in your decision and at what price point to list your Air Bnb in the Seattle Area.
